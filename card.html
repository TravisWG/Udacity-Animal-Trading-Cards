Cat

Razor sharp claws and ninja-like reflexes make the common house cat one of the most notorious predators in the animal kingdom

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 18 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Random boxes

Coming in many differents colors, house cats are among the most adorable creatures on the internet. They reach about 18 inches in length, and are known for being equal parts stupid, cuddly, and vicious.
